About me

Bluestar

Poet & Storyteller

I write about things that resist being named — the textures of ordinary moments, the weight of unsaid words, the places where feeling and language quietly part ways.

Some nights the words come easily. Other nights, the silence is the poem. I write in both English and Hindi — sometimes a feeling only fits one language.

Based in Bengaluru. Writing since forever. Publishing since now.
